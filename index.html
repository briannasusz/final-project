Cyber Bullying

What is it?
The use of electronic communicatuion to taunt and insult a person.
Typically over social media, these messages are usually intimidating or threatening.

How to identify it?
Usually the child or person being cyberbullied will be too ashamed to tell someone they are being bullied.
That's why if an allogation is made an investigation must be done to collect all evidence.

How to prevent it?
Restrict access on social media, or monitor your childs activity on social media.
Also, prolongning pr preventing your childs access to technology.
Such as computers or cellular devices will make cyberbullying impossible towards your child, or prevent hem from bullying other kids.
